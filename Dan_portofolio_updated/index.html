About Me

Hi, my name’s Dan Seilheimer. I’m a UX designer, animator, and illustrator from San Antonio Texas. My passion is bridging the gap between functionality and aesthetics. This interest has lead me to do many different types of projects and acquire a wide array of skills along the way, from animating, designing and programming games, doing UX design work for non-profits like Respite Care of San Antonio, and tons of personal and freelance work. I have a degree in Animation from Northwest Vista College and a certificate in UXUI design from the University of Texas at San Antonio.
